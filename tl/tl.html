About SNOW

SNOW is a visual novel released in 2004, with various editions released afterwards. It was developed by Studio Mebius, which I'm not even sure still exists (more than just legally, anyhow). This project is based on the "Plus Edition" (PE) released in 2006, supporting a whopping 800x600 resolution and with two new routes introduced in the various console releases.

There is another version of SNOW named the "Standard Edition" which is PE plus additional fandisc content, with quite horrifying contents in my opinion. This, plus the fact that I couldn't even get it technically working with the patchwork of ancient tools supporting this visual novel's engine, are why PE is being used.

It was only released in Japanese obviously, which is why there is a fan-driven effort to translate it into English.


The Team

We have named ourselves Snowy Ryujin TL. I'm one of the translators and the one who got the ball rolling on this project. Rinny is the "manager" in a sense, Takafumi is doing image editing, Tsukishiro is another translator, and waffletown is editing the transations.
